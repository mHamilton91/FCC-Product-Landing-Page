The Smarter Way to Learn and Play Music

Play, Jam, Learn


With Lumi Keys you can play along, note by note, following the lights, at your own pace to your favorite songs. The chords and notes in the song will also light up so you can improvise in real time. There are also fun interactive lessons that you can take advantage of to increase your fundamental music skills.

Unique Illuminated Keys, Modular, Compact, & Lightweight


With the full-color illuminated keys, Lumi Keys can be played anywhere, even outdoors. The illumination is not the only advantage of Lumi Keys, the keys are also designed to a custom size of DS 5.5 which is optimized for the average human hand. If you need a larger keyboard to practice and create your music, you can snap two or more Lumi Keys together to instantly expand your keyboard. With the Lumi Keys weighing just at 600g, it is compact and lightweight design makes it portable. When you're taking Lumi on the go, you can protect the keyboard in style with a custom-made Snapcase

LUMI App


Lumi Keys is powered by the Lumi app. Within the app you can select a song that you like and choose what part you would like to play, whether it be the melody, the chords, or the bassline. There are different views for reading music, ranging from a simple colorful stream of notes, all the way to reading sheet music as you progress through your music journey. This allows you to play at your own pace and slowly increase until you are playing at the true tempo of the song.
